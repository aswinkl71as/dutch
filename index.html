Story of Dutch America

We are a top-notch B2B marketplace of FMCG (Fast moving consumable goods) products that link manufacturers and sellers. Dutch America served over 8000 customers in Bahrain with a wide variety of wholesale FMCG products. We have teamed up with multiple reputed brands that are number one in producing high-quality FMCG products.

We define success by the satisfaction of our customers. So we always try to provide a more convenient shopping experience to our customers.

1. Quality and affordable services

We strive to provide quality products and exceptional customer service to maintain our dignity and relevant position in the market.

2. Customer Oriented

We encourage our staff to serve the customers in the best possible way and fulfill their every necessity.

3. Trustworthy and Punctual

We have gained the unbreakable trust of our customers with on-time, safe, and responsible delivery.

Logistics Delivery
